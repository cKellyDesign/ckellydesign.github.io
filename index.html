ABOUT

My name is Conor Kelly and I strive to design, discuss, explore, and ultimately build ICT solutions utilizing HCD methods to advance efforts made to solve some of society's most difficult problems. I am a designer, a coder, a problem solver who is passionate about creating positive social impact and empowering / enriching the lives of my users.

Human Centered Designer

“Form follows function” is cornerstone design principle and I believe that function follows human users, with focus focus I strive to build empathy and iterate approaches when searching for solutions.

UX Researcher

With questionnaires and UX data analytics we can see WHAT works but using qualitative data collection and analysis we can start to postulate the WHY. With these insights we try and try again.

Innovative Developer

With the plethora of ever expanding tools at my disposal, nothing is impossible. I constantly strive to produce efficient, scalable solutions to complex and diverse problems.
